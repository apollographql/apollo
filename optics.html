What’s included in the Optics free trial?

The Optics free trial is a fully-featured version of the Optics Plus edition that lasts for 14 days. No credit card is required to signup for the free trial. At the end of the 14 day trial period, users can either continue using a paid edition of Optics (Essentials or Plus) or the free Developer edition.

What counts as a GraphQL request?

Any API request received by a GraphQL server that is instrumented by Optics counts toward an account’s total monthly request volume.

What happens if I exceed my monthly request volume limit?

By default, Optics free trials that convert to paid editions begin at the lowest price tier for that edition. If your paid Optics account exceeds a monthly request volume tier, your account will be automatically upgraded to the next tier and billed accordingly.

Is there a free edition of Optics available?

At the end of the Optics free trial period, users can either continue using a paid edition of Optics (Essentials or Plus) or the free Developer edition. Users can also downgrade from a paid edition of Optics to the free Developer edition.

Can I cancel?

By default, Optics is billed monthly and you can cancel at any time from within your Optics account. Users can also downgrade from a paid edition of Optics to the free Developer edition.

Is discounted annual pricing available?

By default, Optics is billed as a monthly subscription. If you would like to pre-pay for an annual Optics subscription, you will receive a discount for annual billing. For annual billing options, please contact sales
